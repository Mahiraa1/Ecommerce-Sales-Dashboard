E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## About the Project
I built this Power BI dashboard to analyze e-commerce sales data and gain insights into sales performance, profitability, customer contributions, and product trends.
The goal of this project was to practice data visualization, dashboard design, and business analysis using Power BI while creating an interactive report that helps answer key business questions.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel

## Dashboard Highlights
The dashboard includes:
- Total Sales, Profit, Profit % and Units Sold KPIs
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- Country-wise Sales Performance
- Sales by Business Segment
- Product-wise Sales Distribution
- Top Customers by Sales
- Monthly Sales and Profit Comparison
- Year and Month Filters for interactive analysis

## Key Insights
- Government segment generated the highest sales.
- Japan was the top-performing country in terms of revenue.
- Sales peaked during July.
- Paseo contributed the highest share of units sold.
- A small group of customers contributed a significant portion of overall sales.

The dashboard provides a centralized view of sales and profitability metrics, helping businesses monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions efficiently.
